Principal

Sengunthar College of Engineering, one more feather in the crown of the Sengunthar Charitable Trust, has commenced its maiden voyage from the year 2007 - 2008 with a crew to work for the upliftment of the women students in the world arena and later in the year 2010 - 2011 it has been converted into Co-education college for the benefit of the rural students.

It is started with the ambition of providing high class and modern education to compete the developing hi-tech and quality based educational society. The college has been approved by AICTE, New Delhi and affiliated to Anna University of Technology, Chennai.

Sengunthar Charitable Trust

Sengunthar Charitable Trust is registered in the year 1995. The trust has established its niche in the field of education. The trust's venture "Sengunthar Engineering College" is dedicated and committed to quality and excellence in Engineering and technology pursuits. It renders its reliable services to the national cause of improving the quality of life of our people with importance to the betterment of rural people and their economic development . The college has reputation for high standards of discipline.

Tiruchengode is an entrepreneurship town with a cluster of textile mills, lorry building workshops and rig works. All modern facilities are available in this town and it is well connected to important cities like Erode (20Kms), Salem(45Kms), Namakkal(35Kms), Karur(55Kms), in all directions. The textiles manufactured in this region hold very good export market.

Away from the crowd and the don bustle of heavy vehicular traffic, silhouetted against a picturesque and panoramic landscape where one can see replete with buildings, the college is located at the foothill of Nagagiri and the Shrine dedicated to Lord Arthanareeswara which is situated at the top of the hill. The College is at a distance of 3Kms from Tiruchengode on the Tiruchengode-Namakkal road in Kosavampalayam with a landscape of 45 acres.
